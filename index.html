What is included with my solution?

For all clusters, a standard cluster stack will be included. This includes cluster management and monitoring tools, a scheduler, compilers and libraries (including MPI), and environment modules.

For all storage servers, a filesystem and requested partition(s), and associated utilities may be included.

For all solutions with GPUs, a standard AI Software Stack, and associated GPU libraries will be included.

The standard workstation will include one OS operating system and one application installed. Dual boot or additional applications will fall under our hourly engineering time rate for additional installs. These systems will not be available for remote testing access as they straight forward in regards to the electronic SOW.

The standard server will include one OS operating system and two applications installed. Dual boot or additional applications will fall under our hourly engineering time rate for additional installs. These systems will not be available for remote testing access as they straight forward in regards to the electronic SOW.

The standard cluster will include one OS operating system and up to three applications installed. Dual boot or additional applications will fall under our hourly engineering time rate for additional installs. These systems will be available for remote testing access as we encourage factory floor remote testing regarding the electronic SOW for verification.

The large-scale-out systems or high-density AI systems, including storage, the software stack will be tailored to the opportunity by mutual agreement through the electronic SOW.
